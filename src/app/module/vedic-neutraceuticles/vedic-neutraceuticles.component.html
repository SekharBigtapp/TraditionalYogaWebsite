Vedic Nutraceuticals (VN) provides high quality products to support healthy living, informed by ancient sciences of Yoga and Ayurveda.

We believe in preventative health and work on the principle that every single input to the body matters. For these reasons, VN products are designed to support the practice of all limbs of yoga, including aspects such as cleansing, muscle building, respiratory health and meditation.

From the nutrition that we consume, to the water we drink and the air we breathe, everything has an effect on how we feel, how we live and who we are. Yoga is about conservation not consumption, about quality and not quantity. When we are healthy and happy we have less desire to consume. As a company established and run by volunteers, our wish is to facilitate and empower people to care for every cell in their bodies.

Vedic Neutraceuticals is a non-profit company owned and run by the volunteers of Traditional yoga. A very small mark-up on products generates funding which supports continuous production and development of products to the very highest stanards.
